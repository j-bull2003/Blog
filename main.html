My Journey to Becoming a Software Engineer

A Degree Isn't Always Necessary:

How I passed the Coding Interview

Hello everyone, my name is Jessica and I am a 19-year-old full-stack software engineer and a university student. I know that in the tech industry, a college degree is often seen as a requirement, but I am here to prove that it is possible to achieve success without one. In this blog post, I will be sharing my story and the lessons I have learned along the way.

I started by working on personal projects and learning new programming languages. I also sought out mentorship from more experienced developers. I applied to many software engineering jobs and finally landed my current job while still being a student. I learned a lot during my time as a software engineer, like how to work in a team, how to communicate effectively and how to manage time efficiently.
